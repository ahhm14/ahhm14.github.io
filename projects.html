Machine Learning Methods for Smart Grid Operation with LECs

PhD Thesis | Universitat Politècnica de Catalunya | 2025

Doctoral thesis examining how machine learning can improve operations in decentralised electricity systems incorporating Local Energy Communities (LECs). Analyses the integration of LECs with smart distribution grids and develops predictive frameworks for managing line congestion in complex power systems with renewable energy sources and prosumers. Awarded an International Doctorate mention.

Radiomics for Cardiovascular Disease Diagnosis

Master's Thesis | University of Barcelona (UB)

Final Master's Project for the MSc in Fundamentals of Data Science at UB. Applies radiomics techniques to cardiac magnetic resonance (CMR) imaging for cardiovascular disease diagnosis, combining hand-crafted radiomics features with deep learning-based feature extraction from convolutional neural networks. Includes a feature fusion pipeline evaluated on the ACDC Challenge and UK BioBank benchmark datasets.
